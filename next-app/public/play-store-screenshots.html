What is the recommended oxytocin protocol for active management of third stage of labour?

Active Management of Third Stage (AMTSL)

Preferred: Oxytocin 10 IU IM immediately after birth

If unavailable: Misoprostol 600 mcg orally

Reduces PPH risk by 60% vs expectant management.

✅ Safe: Oxytocin 10 IU IM (first-line)
